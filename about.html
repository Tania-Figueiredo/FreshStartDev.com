Hi, I’m Tânia Figueiredo

I build human-centred digital experiences — combining design, empathy and clean code to turn ideas into real products.

My path into tech

Before becoming a developer, I worked in financial consultancy — a field that taught me precision, analytical thinking, and empathy for people’s goals.

Over time, I realised I could bring those same principles into technology. I wanted to create tools that make people’s lives easier, clearer, and more meaningful.

That’s how FreshStart Dev was born — a portfolio product I designed and developed from scratch to show what I can build: from UI/UX and front-end architecture to product structure and communication.

What I do best

Focused but versatile.

  • Frontend development: React, Angular and WordPress — building clear, responsive interfaces.
  • Integration & automation: connecting front-end with APIs, CRMs, and payment systems.
  • UX/UI design: turning ideas into intuitive, accessible experiences.
  • Product ownership: translating goals into structure, documentation, and measurable results.

Growth & Community

I believe growth happens through people and learning. In November 2025, I’ll be joining the WebSummit in Lisbon as a volunteer — to learn from innovators, connect with the community, and keep building forward.

If you’re looking for someone who understands both technology and people — let’s build something meaningful together.
